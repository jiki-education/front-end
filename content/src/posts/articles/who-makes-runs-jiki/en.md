Jiki is run by [Jeremy Walker](https://ihid.info), and has been created by Jeremy, [Aron Demeter](https://www.linkedin.com/in/aron-demeter-6580391b8/) and [Nicole Chalmers](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nicole-chalmers/).

Jiki came out of [Exercism](https://exercism.org), a nonprofit created by [Katrina Owen](https://exercism.github.io/kytrinyx/) and now led by Jeremy. The platform has largely been built by Jeremy, Aron and [Erik Schierboom](https://www.erikschierboom.com/), with thousands of contributions from our community. Its content is created by [a huge team of volunteers](https://exercism.org/contributing/contributors) including our [dedicated team of maintainers](https://exercism.org/contributing/contributors?category=maintaining).
