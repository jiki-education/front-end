---
title: "Bouncer"
description: "Decide who's old enough to get into the club."
---

You're a bouncer at a venue. Your job is to check someone's age and decide whether to open the door.

You have two functions:

- `getAge()` returns the age of the person waiting outside
- `openDoor()` opens the door to let them in

The venue's policy is that only people **over 20** (not including 20) are allowed in. Get the person's age, and if they're old enough, open the door!
