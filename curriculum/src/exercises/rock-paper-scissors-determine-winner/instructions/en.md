title: "Rock, Paper, Scissors: Determine Winner"
description: "Determine the winner of a Rock Paper Scissors game."
---

To start our game of Rock, Paper, Scissors, we're going to write some code to determine the winner.

In Rock, Paper, Scissors, two players each choose rock, paper or scissors. In our game, the two players are **Yuki** and **Ando**. You compare their choices to see who's won:

- If both players choose the same, it's a tie.
- Rock blunts scissors (rock wins).
- Scissors cut paper (scissors wins).
- Paper smothers rock (paper wins).

Your job is to compare the choices Yuki and Ando have made and announce the winner to the playing hall.
