This exercise introduces you to the `rectangle` function.

### Drawing Rectangles

To draw rectangles, we use the `rectangle` function with 4 inputs:

- `left`: The left side of the rectangle
- `top`: The top of the rectangle
- `width`: How wide the rectangle is
- `height`: How high the rectangle is

## Instructions

Your job is to use three rectangles to fill the holes in the wall.

The top-left of the drawing canvas is `0,0`. The bottom-right is `100,100`. You can hover over the canvas to check where something needs to go.

To make your life easier, the top, left, height and widths are all divisible by 10.
