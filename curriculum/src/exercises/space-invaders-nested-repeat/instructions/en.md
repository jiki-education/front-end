Even more aliens have appeared, and now they're **four rows deep**!

You have the same two functions as before:

- `move()` moves your laser cannon one position to the right
- `shoot()` fires the laser upwards

There's still a pattern to the alien positions, but this time you'll need to use a **repeat loop inside another repeat loop** (a nested loop) to destroy them all efficiently.

Your solution must be **7 lines of code or fewer**.
