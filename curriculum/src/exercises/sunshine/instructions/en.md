This exercise introduces you to the `triangle` and `circle` functions.

### Drawing Triangles

To draw triangles, we use the `triangle` function with 7 inputs:

- `x1`, `y1`: The first corner point
- `x2`, `y2`: The second corner point
- `x3`, `y3`: The third corner point
- `color`: The color of the triangle (e.g. `"yellow"`)

### Drawing Circles

To draw circles, we use the `circle` function with 4 inputs:

- `x`: The x position of the center of the circle
- `y`: The y position of the center of the circle
- `radius`: How big the circle is
- `color`: The color of the circle (e.g. `"yellow"`)

## Instructions

We've drawn a `"skyblue"` sky for you.

Your job is to draw the sunshine by adding 8 `"yellow"` triangle spikes around the canvas and a `"yellow"` circle in the center.

The top-left of the drawing canvas is `0,0`. The bottom-right is `100,100`. You can hover over the canvas to check coordinates.
