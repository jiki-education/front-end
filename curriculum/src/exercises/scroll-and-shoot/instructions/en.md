Welcome to the first Space Invaders exercise. This is a classic 1970s game, and one of the first I ever played!

By the end of the Bootcamp you'll have built this from scratch. But for now, your job is to play and win the game, by shooting down all the aliens.

You can move the laser left and right using the `moveLeft()` and `moveRight()` functions. You can experiment to see how far left and right you can move. If you go off the edge of the screen, you lose.

As you move, you need to check whether there's an alien above you using the `isAlienAbove()` function and then `shoot()` it if so. If you shoot when there's not an alien, you'll lose the game - wasting ammo is not allowed!

The laser canon easily overheats. You need to move between shoots to keep it cool.

Once all the aliens have been shot down, you win!
