The aliens are back, and there are more of them this time!

You have the same two functions as before:

- `move()` moves your laser cannon one position to the right
- `shoot()` fires the laser upwards

But this time, there's a pattern to the alien positions. Look carefully at where they are and use a **repeat loop** to destroy them all efficiently.

Your solution must be **7 lines of code or fewer**.
