---
title: "Traffic Lights"
description: "Draw a traffic light using variables for position and size."
---

Your task is to complete the traffic light by drawing the three colored lights using the <define>variables</define> provided.

We've done the work of working out the positions for you. You just need to draw the circles using the right variables and the right colors.

The variables we've created are:

- <define>`radius`</define> — the radius of each light
- <define>`centerX`</define> — the horizontal center of all three lights
- <define>`topY`</define> — the vertical center of the top (red) light
- <define>`middleY`</define> — the vertical center of the middle (amber) light
- <define>`bottomY`</define> — the vertical center of the bottom (green) light

You should use `"red"`, `"amber"` and `"green"` for the colors.

Good luck!
