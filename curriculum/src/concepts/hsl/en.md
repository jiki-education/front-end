---
title: "HSL Colors"
description: "Specifying colors using Hue, Saturation and Lightness values."
---

HSL stands for Hue, Saturation, and Lightness. It's an intuitive way to specify colors:

- **Hue**: The color (0-360, like a color wheel)
- **Saturation**: How vivid the color is (0-100%)
- **Lightness**: How light or dark (0-100%)
