---
title: "Using Functions"
description: "Calling functions to make things happen. The first step in programming."
---

Using functions is the most basic thing you do in programming. You write the function's name followed by parentheses, and it does something for you.

For example, `move()` moves a character, `turnLeft()` turns it left. You don't need to know how they work inside - just call them and they do their thing.
